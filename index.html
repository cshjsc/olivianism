Welcome to Olivianism

Where the Olive Symbolizes the Eternal Circle of Life

The Path of the Olive

Olivianism is a spiritual journey that embraces the olive as a sacred symbol of life's eternal cycle. Through its branches, leaves, and fruit, we find wisdom, peace, and the interconnectedness of all things.

The Sacred Commandments

First Commandment

Value all life, for all living things are part of the same cycle.

Second Commandment

Respect your harvest and the harvest of others, for no fruit grows without labor.

Third Commandment

Care for the land, for it sustains you as a mother sustains her child.

Fourth Commandment

Honor your elders, for their wisdom is the root of your strength.

Fifth Commandment

Do not waste, for what is given to you must be used with gratitude.

Sixth Commandment

Speak truthfully, and let your word be as clear as pressed oil.

Seventh Commandment

Resolve conflict with peace, as storms destroy but calm brings growth.

Eighth Commandment

Be patient in hardship, as the olive tree bears fruit only in its season.

Ninth Commandment

Welcome the stranger, for all walk the same earth.

Tenth Commandment

Observe rest and reflection, for stillness nourishes the soul like rain to soil.
